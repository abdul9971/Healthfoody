# User Stories: Smart Food & Health App

This document outlines the functional requirements and user expectations for the Food & Health App, designed to help users build healthier habits through data-driven insights.

---

## 1. Onboarding & Profile Setup

### Health Goal Personalization
- **As a** new user, **I want to** input my health goals, dietary preferences, and allergies during onboarding, **so that** the app filters out irrelevant content and focuses on my specific needs.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
1. User can select from goals (e.g., Weight Loss, Muscle Gain, Managing Diabetes).
2. User can select dietary tags (e.g., Keto, Vegan, Gluten-free).
3. App must validate that at least one goal is selected before proceeding.
- **Priority:** High

### Health Data Integration
- **As a** health-conscious user, **I want to** sync my Apple Health/Google Fit data, **so that** the app can adjust my daily calorie and macro targets based on my actual activity levels.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
1. App requests permission for "Steps," "Active Energy," and "Weight."
2. Data syncs automatically on app launch.
3. A "Last Synced" timestamp is visible in settings.
- **Priority:** High

### Biometric Baseline
- **As a** user, **I want to** enter my current weight, height, and age, **so that** the app can calculate my Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) for accurate tracking.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
1. Input fields support both Metric (kg/cm) and Imperial (lbs/ft) units.
2. The app calculates and displays the Daily Caloric Intake goal immediately after input.
- **Priority:** Medium

---

## 2. Food Logging

### AI Visual Recognition
- **As a** busy user, **I want to** take a photo of my meal, **so that** the app can automatically identify food items and estimate portion sizes without manual typing.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
1. Camera interface identifies at least 3 distinct food items in a single frame.
2. User can manually override or confirm the AI-suggested portion size.
3. Processing time for image recognition must be under 3 seconds.
- **Priority:** High

### Quick-Log Favorites
- **As a** routine-driven user, **I want to** save frequent meals as "Favorites," **so that** I can log my daily breakfast or lunch in a single tap.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
1. A "Heart" icon exists next to logged meals to save them.
2. Users can name the favorite (e.g., "Post-Gym Protein Shake").
3. Logging a favorite updates the daily total instantly.
- **Priority:** Medium

### Barcode Scanner
- **As a** grocery shopper, **I want to** scan product barcodes, **so that** I can instantly see the nutritional breakdown and health grade of packaged foods.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
1. Scanner must access a global food database.
2. If a product is not found, the user is prompted to take a photo of the nutrition label for OCR processing.
- **Priority:** High

---

## 3. Smart Recommendations

### Context-Aware Dining Suggestions
- **As a** user eating out, **I want to** receive healthy menu recommendations based on my current location (GPS), **so that** I can stay on track with my goals even at restaurants.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
1. App identifies nearby restaurants using Location Services.
2. App highlights "Top 3 Healthy Picks" from the specific restaurant's menu.
3. Suggestions must align with the user's saved dietary preferences.
- **Priority:** High

### "Swap-This-For-That" Nudges
- **As a** user logging an unhealthy item, **I want** the app to suggest a healthier alternative, **so that** I can learn to make better choices in real-time.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
1. Triggered when a "Red Grade" food (high sugar/sat-fat) is searched.
2. Suggests an alternative with better macro-nutrients (e.g., "Try Greek Yogurt instead of Sour Cream").
- **Priority:** Medium

### Grocery List Generator
- **As a** home cook, **I want** the app to generate a weekly grocery list based on my recommended meal plan, **so that** I have the right ingredients to succeed.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
1. List items are automatically categorized by aisle (Produce, Dairy, etc.).
2. Users can check off items as they shop.
- **Priority:** Low

### Smart Hydration Forecast
- **As a** user, **I want** the app to increase my water intake goal based on high local temperature or intense workouts, **so that** I stay hydrated during peak stress.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
1. Integrates with Weather API to detect heatwaves (>30°C).
2. Detects "Workout" activity from synced fitness data.
3. Automatically adds 500ml to the daily water goal when conditions are met.
- **Priority:** Medium

---

## 4. Habit Analysis & Insights

### Trigger Identification
- **As a** user, **I want to** log my mood or "why" I am eating, **so that** the app can identify patterns of emotional eating.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
1. A simple emoji-based mood selector appears during meal logging.
2. Weekly report correlates "High Stress" with "High Sugar Intake."
- **Priority:** Medium

### Weekly Nutrition Deep-Dive
- **As a** data-driven user, **I want** a weekly summary of my macro and micro-nutrient trends, **so that** I can see where I am consistently lacking.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
1. Visual charts showing Carb/Protein/Fat distribution.
2. A "Top Deficiencies" section highlighting missing vitamins/minerals.
- **Priority:** High

### Time-of-Day Analysis
- **As a** user, **I want to** see at what time of day I consume the most calories, **so that** I can adjust my eating window to prevent late-night snacking.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
1. A heat map or timeline view showing calorie distribution over 24 hours.
2. Comparative insight provided: "You consume 40% of your calories after 8 PM."
- **Priority:** Low

---

## 5. Progress & Health Score

### Holistic Health Score
- **As a** user, **I want** a daily "Health Score" (0–100), **so that** I have a single metric to understand my overall performance across diet, hydration, and activity.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
1. Algorithm weighs Calories (30%), Macro Balance (30%), Hydration (20%), and Steps (20%).
2. Score updates in real-time as data is logged.
- **Priority:** High

### Visual Weight Trend
- **As a** user tracking weight loss, **I want to** see a trend line of my weight over time against my target, **so that** I don't get discouraged by daily fluctuations.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
1. A line graph showing "Moving Average" to smooth out spikes.
2. Percentage of goal completion is displayed (e.g., "60% of the way to your goal").
- **Priority:** Medium

### Streak & Milestone Badges
- **As a** user, **I want to** earn badges for consistency, **so that** I stay motivated through gamification.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
1. Notification triggers when a streak milestone (e.g., 7 days) is hit.
2. A "Trophy Cabinet" section is accessible in the profile.
- **Priority:** Low

---

## 6. Notifications & Reminders

### Contextual Meal Reminders
- **As a** forgetful user, **I want to** receive a notification if I haven't logged a meal 1 hour after my usual eating time, **so that** my data remains accurate.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
1. App learns usual eating times based on historical data.
2. Push notification is triggered if no log is detected within the 1-hour buffer.
- **Priority:** Medium

### The "Grocery Store" Nudge
- **As a** shopper, **I want** a reminder to check my 'Healthiest Alternatives' list when the app detects I am at a grocery store, **so that** I avoid impulsive junk food purchases.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
1. Geofencing triggers a notification when entering a supermarket.
2. Notification links directly to the user's shopping list.
- **Priority:** Low
